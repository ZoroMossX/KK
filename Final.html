I · of · III
the bouquet we carried
✦
My dearest,
If you ever stop and look back at us, maybe you'll see what I'm only now beginning to understand. This bouquet we carried without noticing… it was never just flowers. It was us, in pieces, in colors, in meanings we never paused to name. The tulip was always there, quietly holding our honesty. Every chaotic moment, every argument, every messy truth we didn't hide from each other… it was real. Nothing polished, nothing fake. Just us, saying things as they were, even when it wasn't easy. And then the orchid… something rare, something you don't come across twice. That kind of bond people talk about but don't always find. We did. We held it, maybe carelessly at times, but it was ours. Irreplaceable, even when we didn't say it out loud. Like peonies, we built memories without realizing they'd become something so warm, so permanent. Maybe you've forgotten a few… I'll blame old age for that, don't worry. I remember enough for both of us. The daisy… that was us at our purest. No pretending, no masks. Just a friendship that didn't need effort to feel real. And the lily… trust. The kind where you gave me a name out of nowhere, "Suhani," like it belonged to me all along. The kind where I knew things about you I was never meant to misuse… like a password that somehow meant more than just letters.

II · of · III
roses, sunflowers & everything
✦
Two red roses… because one was never enough. Love, care, appreciation… not loud, not dramatic, but constant. And the sunflowers… I never really liked flowers before. They felt temporary, like something pretty that fades too soon. But maybe I never understood them. Maybe I never had one that stayed. Maybe… just maybe… you were my sunflower. Even if someday you slowly fade into distance… I think a part of me will still be looking for you in small things. Not in a way that holds you back, but in a way that quietly keeps you with me.
something that became everything 🤍
I want you to live every life you dream of. Every version of happiness, every path you choose, even if it takes you far away from me. Some things never needed to be said out loud… yet they stayed, steady and certain, in ways that don't fade. And in all those days that felt ordinary at the time… something settled without asking to be noticed… …and somehow, it became everything.
with all of this, always —
✦   suhani   ✦
